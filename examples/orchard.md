# Example: Orchard Inspection

## User Input

Scenario: orchard  
Crop: apple trees  
Tree age: 5 years  
Weather: recent heatwave after a wet spring  
Irrigation: micro-sprinklers, twice weekly  
Observation: leaves on the south side of several trees look curled and pale  

## Expected Agent Focus

- Compare affected and healthy sides of the canopy.
- Check whether symptoms align with sun exposure.
- Review irrigation coverage and soil moisture.
- Inspect leaf undersides for pests.
- Ask whether symptoms appear on new growth, older leaves, or fruit.

## First Report Summary

Possible causes include heat stress, irrigation non-uniformity, nutrient stress, pest pressure, or root-zone issues after wet conditions. The first action should be inspection and comparison, not treatment.
