# Safety Disclaimer

AI Farm Copilot is an open-source inspection and decision-support project. It is designed to help users organize field observations and generate practical next steps.

It is not a certified agronomist, pesticide adviser, turf expert, irrigation engineer, veterinarian, attorney, or local compliance authority.

## Important Limits

- Visual analysis may be incomplete or wrong.
- Similar symptoms can have different causes.
- Local pests, diseases, soil conditions, water quality, and regulations vary widely.
- Chemical, fertilizer, irrigation, and crop-loss decisions can carry safety, legal, financial, and environmental consequences.

## Use Professional Review For

- Pesticide, herbicide, fungicide, and chemical decisions.
- Disease confirmation.
- Major irrigation changes.
- Commercial crop-loss situations.
- High-value turf or landscape decisions.
- Local regulatory compliance.
- Worker safety and environmental safety.

## Recommended Use

Use AI Farm Copilot to:

- Create scouting notes.
- Structure field observations.
- Identify what to inspect next.
- Compare possible causes.
- Prepare a report for a local expert.
- Track changes over time.

Do not use AI Farm Copilot as the only basis for high-risk treatment decisions.
