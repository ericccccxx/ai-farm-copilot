Observations

Likely Causes

Water stressHeat, wind, and leaf curl make this a medium-likelihood cause.
Nutrient stressLower leaf yellowing can match nutrient imbalance or deficiency.
Irrigation uniformitySymptoms may follow drip line variation or emitter issues.

Recommended Next Actions

  1. Check soil moisture at root depth in affected and healthy zones.
  2. Inspect nearby drip emitters or sprinkler coverage.
  3. Capture comparison photos of healthy and affected plants or turf.
  4. Review fertilizer, spray, mowing, pruning, or treatment history.
  5. Recheck the same location within 48-72 hours.

Do Not Do Yet

  • Do not make chemical or major irrigation decisions from a single AI report.
  • Escalate to a qualified local expert if the issue spreads, affects high-value areas, or requires treatment.
